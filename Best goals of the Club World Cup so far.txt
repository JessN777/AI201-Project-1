Best goals of the Club World Cup so far
💬Discussion
We're through the group stage and there have been some absolute bangers. Here's my personal top 5 — drop yours below.

1. Vinicius Jr. vs Palmeiras — bicycle kick from the edge of the box, completely out of nowhere. Keeper had no chance.

2. Mbappe vs Bayern — picked it up 40 yards out, drove at the defense, cut inside and curled it into the top corner. Pure class.

3. Bruno Fernandes vs Al Hilal — whipped free kick that dipped at the last second. Keeper went the right way but it was unhittable.

4. Erling Haaland vs Auckland City — okay it was basically a tap-in but he celebrated like he'd just scored in a World Cup final so it makes the list for entertainment value alone.

5. Pedro vs Chelsea — composed finish after a 60-yard team move by Flamengo. Eight passes, every one of them perfect.

Honorable mention to that Kimmich long-range thunderbolt. If it goes in instead of rattling the bar it might be goal of the tournament.

What's everyone else's list?
