Club World Cup Group Stage Results Day 3
📰News
Here is a summary of all Day 3 matches from the FIFA Club World Cup group stage:

Group A:
- Chelsea 3 - 1 Flamengo
- Palmeiras 2 - 2 Al Hilal

Group B:
- Manchester City 5 - 0 Auckland City
- Juventus 1 - 0 Urawa Red Diamonds

Group C:
- Real Madrid 2 - 1 Al Ahly
- Inter Miami 1 - 3 Bayern Munich

Group D:
- PSG 4 - 0 Mamelodi Sundowns
- Atletico Madrid 2 - 2 River Plate

Standout performer of the day was Erling Haaland with a brace for Man City, bringing his tournament tally to 5 goals in just 3 games. Lionel Messi started on the bench for Inter Miami but came on in the second half to a thunderous ovation from the crowd in Miami.
