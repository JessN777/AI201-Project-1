Knockout stage fantasy advice — here's who I'm targeting and who I'm avoiding.

MUST HAVES:
- Mbappe (Real Madrid) — in the form of his life, guaranteed starter, plays every minute
- Haaland (Man City) — 6 goals in the group stage, draws fouls, takes penalties
- Vinicius Jr. (Real Madrid) — assists and goals, probably the most creative player in the tournament right now

GOOD VALUE PICKS:
- Pedro (Flamengo) — if you need budget relief he's been the best South American player here
- Iqraam Rayners (Mamelodi Sundowns) — long shot but cheap and has been scoring
- Kimmich (Bayern) — points from defense, set pieces, and he nearly scored that screamer

AVOID:
- Any Inter Miami player not named Messi — the squad around him just isn't consistent enough for knockout football
- Goalkeepers from teams facing Real Madrid or City — they will concede

General tip: Stack your team from whichever side of the bracket looks easier. Real Madrid and City are on opposite sides, so pick one and go heavy on that team's attackers.

Good luck everyone.
