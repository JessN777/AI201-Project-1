Is Inter Miami actually a contender or just a Messi show?
💬Discussion
Genuine question — can Inter Miami go deep in the Club World Cup or are they just here for the spectacle?

They've got Messi obviously, but the squad depth compared to Real Madrid, City or Bayern is nowhere close. In their opening match against Al Ahly they looked shaky at the back and relied entirely on Messi to create anything going forward.

Arguments for them being a real threat:
- Messi on form can beat any team on any day
- They've been building the squad around him for 2 years now
- Home crowd support in Miami could be massive

Arguments against:
- The MLS level of competition doesn't prepare you for Bayern or City
- Their defense is genuinely concerning
- No proven big-game backup if Messi is neutralized

My prediction: They make it out of the group stage by the skin of their teeth, then get absolutely demolished by whoever finishes top of Group B. Change my mind.
