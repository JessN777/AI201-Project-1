Club World Cup attendance and viewership numbers are insane
📰News
FIFA has released the early attendance and viewership figures for the 2025 Club World Cup and they are legitimately impressive.

Attendance highlights:
- Average attendance per match: 52,000
- Highest single match crowd: 85,000 (Real Madrid vs Inter Miami at MetLife Stadium)
- Total attendance through group stage: Over 2.1 million

TV viewership:
- Global broadcast reach: 180+ countries
- US viewership up 340% compared to the old 7-team format
- Streaming numbers broke records on every platform carrying the tournament

Revenue:
- FIFA projected $1 billion in revenue — early estimates suggest they'll surpass that
- Merchandise sales in the US alone are triple what FIFA projected

The tournament has been a genuine commercial success and is already being pointed to as a preview of what the 2026 World Cup will look like in the same host cities. Critics who said the expanded format was a cash grab may need to eat some humble pie — the fans clearly showed up.
